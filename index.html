    Our Team
    Elwin Li
    Co-Founder, Executive Director
    Elwin is the Executive Director and Co-founder of Kosumi. He has been playing go for 10 years, and has represented the US in the 2018 World Youth Go Championships, getting 5th place. As the main teacher of Kosumi, he hopes to share his passion for the game with his students.
    Amanda Zhang
    Co-Founder, Outreach Director
    Amanda is Kosumi's Outreach Director and a junior at Mountain View High School, where she is also involved in the Go club. She fell in love with the game a few summers ago and hopes to help other players do the same.
    Alan Chian
    Treasurer
    Alan is Kosumi's finance manager and a junior at Mountain View high school. He has played go starting from the age of 2 and has been a pro for 14 years. He is also president of the go club at Mountain View High School, and has previously competed in the 2020 World Go Championships. In the tough environment of zoom, he received 1st place. Alan hopes that one day all of Kosumi's students will be like him and aspire to become world changing go players.
    Mark Zhang
    Teacher
    Ever since his first game a few years ago during a chance encounter with a 5 dan on a stormy November evening, Mark has desired to spread awareness about go in America. He hopes to share his interest with the students at Kosumi and guide them along their go-playing journey.
    Sander Vonk
    Technology and Media Officer
    Sander, also a junior at Mountain View High School, has never played go a day in his life. He serves as the manager of media, online presence, and all things technological at Kosumi.
